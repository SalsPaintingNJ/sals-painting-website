Process

How interior painting jobs normally move

Step 1

Walk the room.

Confirm the surfaces, current condition, and whether any drywall repair or trim touchup is part of the scope.

Step 2

Prep the surface.

Patch, sand, clean, and prime where needed so the topcoat sits evenly.

Step 3

Paint cleanly.

Apply the selected finish with attention to cut lines, corners, and room transitions.

Step 4

Check the details.

Make sure the room looks complete, not half-finished once the paint dries.
